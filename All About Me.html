My Autobiography!











Hiya! My name is Vashti E. Domingo! I’m 18 years old and I was born on October 1, 2005. I'm currently living with my parents,
Rosalinda E. Domingo, and Renan C. Domingo, in my hometown of General Trias Cavite, in the tropical country of the Philippines. I’m also currently studying in the Lyceum of the Philippines University in Computer Science and, hopefully soon, in Animation and
Digital Arts. It’s kind of funny, actually… because back when I was just in 11th grade, I always thought that I would end up taking Multimedia Arts as my
course, because drawing was pretty much my entire life.









Back when I was a kid (until now, of course), every time I had access to paper and pens, I always sketch and draw for as long as I can
until I get bored or distracted from it. It was pretty much the only thing I knew how to do and was happy in doing it. But after thinking about and talking to my mom about it, I realized that I wanted to branch out of my comfort zone more and learn
new skills that I can incorporate my art with, and to not just stick to knowing how to do just one thing in my life. So, although I had a lot of doubts starting out the first time, I still pushed through all of the hard and frustrating challenges
during my first year, what with all the coding and math and whatnot.





















Honestly, it even got to the point when I started to genuinely think about shifting to another course and just straight-up quitting.

And, thanks to God, my parents, my friends, my classmates, my amazing professors, and their progressively interesting subjects…
Well... Let’s just say I changed my mind, aaand now I’m still here surviving, somehow!

(Seriously, guys, thank you for everything.)

















But enough of that sappy stuff...
